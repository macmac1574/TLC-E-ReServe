About the App

A smarter way to manage reservations

TLC E-ReServe is a Facility Reservation System designed for TLC institutions. It streamlines the way faculty, and administrators reserve facilities, and equipment replacing manual logbooks with a fast, centralized digital workflow.

Purpose

Centralize the entire reservation process from request to approval into a single mobile platform.

Who Can Use It

Faculty and Administrators with assigned tlc email accounts. Admins manage approvals; users submit and track requests.

Benefits

Easy reservations, multi-date reserving, approval workflow, and real-time reservation tracking — all in your pocket.
